Installation Services

Professional installation of fire protection systems tailored to your requirements

Cape Town Fire & Maintenance Service is a leading fire safety solutions provider in South Africa. As part of our services, we offer installation of fire protection systems such as fire alarms, fire extinguishers, sprinkler systems and hydrant systems just to name a few.

At Cape Town Fire & Maintenance Service, our field engineers and technicians are licensed and trained to provide quick, efficient installation of fire protection systems. We take a consultative approach for each project in order to understand our client's requirements and with our "out of the box" thinking also provides innovative cost-effective solutions for your fire protection needs.

Our Approach
